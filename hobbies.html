Hobbies and Interests

I have been playing squash since a very early age and I was a member of the Lancashire junior squash team from the age of ten through to eighteen. My highest individual position in the England Squash National Junior Rankings was 35. I also played for my local club Preston Grasshoppers. I now play for one of the University of Nottingham teams as well as running the IMS Squash Leagues.



I started playing the guitar in primary school. I had lessons up to grade 6 and was a part of the Lancashire Schools Jazz Orchestra.





I also enjoy spending time in the great outdoors. Whether it is camping, walking, cycling or climbing I enjoy lots of activities when being out in the country is an option!
